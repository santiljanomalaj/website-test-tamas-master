[date]13.10.2016[date]

[category]Software[category]

![CAM-processor](../img/blog/cam.png)

[description]
Although the work on the CAM-processor started long time ago, the need of many different features slowed development.</br>
Things like automatic choosing of fits and the automatic creation of comments and check sizes make life so much easier for machine workers.</br>
The creation of NC-programs will be further automated through generation of processing strategies and choosing tools and process parameters.
